# Chapter 06 — The Founding Council

## Why a Council

Categories are not created by companies. They are created by communities.

Salesforce did not create the CRM category alone — it created the ecosystem, the vocabulary, and the community of practitioners that made CRM the standard of record for every sales organization.

The Procurement Intelligence category will be created the same way: by a community of serious practitioners who adopt the framework, demonstrate its value, and establish the vocabulary that the broader market eventually adopts.

The Procurement Intelligence Council is that community.

## Council Structure

### Founding Members (Cohort 1 — 2025)
- Maximum 50 practitioners
- By invitation only
- Criteria: senior BD/capture professional, minimum 5 years government contracting experience, commitment to active participation

### Responsibilities
- Contribute to framework development
- Share (appropriately anonymized) win/loss intelligence
- Publish at least one substantive contribution per quarter
- Participate in quarterly virtual roundtables
- Review and endorse annual State of Procurement Intelligence report

### Benefits
- Early access to UzimaAmka platform (founding member pricing)
- Category co-authorship credit
- Peer network with senior practitioners across the industry
- Speaking opportunities at PIMM (Procurement Intelligence Maturity Model) workshops
- Research contributions to the annual State of Procurement Intelligence report

## Council Governance

The Council is practitioner-governed. A five-member steering committee sets the research agenda, approves new members, and maintains intellectual standards.

UzimaAmka provides infrastructure support (platform, publishing, facilitation) but does not control the intellectual direction. The Council's credibility depends on its independence.

## How to Join

Founding membership applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.

To apply:
1. Email council@uzimaAmka.com with subject: "Founding Council Application"
2. Include your current role, firm, and years in government contracting BD/capture
3. Write 2–3 sentences on why you believe Procurement Intelligence is the right framing for the discipline
4. Describe one specific win or loss that would have gone differently with better pre-solicitation intelligence

Applications are reviewed within 10 business days. Founding cohort closes when 50 members are confirmed.
