# Procurement Intelligence Maturity Model (PIMM)
## Self-Assessment Framework

*Version 1.0 — Procurement Intelligence Council*

---

## Instructions

Score your organization on each of the 25 criteria below using a 1–4 scale:
- **1** — Not practiced / ad hoc
- **2** — Practiced inconsistently / partially
- **3** — Practiced consistently with some gaps
- **4** — Fully institutionalized / best practice

Score each section, then total for your overall PIMM score (25–100).
