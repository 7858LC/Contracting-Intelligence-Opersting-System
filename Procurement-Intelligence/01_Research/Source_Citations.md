# Source Citations & Research Bibliography

## Government Data Sources

### Primary Procurement Data
- **USASpending.gov** — Federal awards, contracts, grants (usaspending.gov)
- **SAM.gov** — Opportunities, entity registration, exclusions (sam.gov)
- **FPDS-NG** — Federal Procurement Data System — Next Generation (fpds.gov)
- **PIEE / Procurement Integrated Enterprise Environment** — DoD contract data
- **SBIR.gov** — Small business innovation research awards

### Agency-Specific Sources
- **Army Contracting Command** — acc.army.mil
- **Naval Sea Systems Command** — navsea.navy.mil  
- **GSA eBuy / eOffer** — buy.gsa.gov
- **HHS PSC** — hhs.gov/grants
- **DHS EAGLE / Alliant** — dhs.gov/procurement

### Congressional & Regulatory
- **FAR / DFARS** — acquisition.gov
- **GAO Bid Protest Decisions** — gao.gov/legal/bid-protests
- **Inspector General Reports** — various agency OIG portals
- **Congressional Budget Justifications** — appropriations.house.gov

## Commercial Intelligence Sources

- **GovWin IQ** (Deltek) — opportunity pipeline and agency intelligence
- **Bloomberg Government** — federal spending analysis
- **HigherGov** — market intelligence and incumbent tracking
- **GovTribe** — contract award trends and NAICS analysis
- **INPUT/Onvia** — state and local procurement data

## Academic & Industry Research

- Professional Services Council (PSC) — Annual industry surveys
- National Defense Industrial Association (NDIA) — Defense acquisition studies
- RAND Corporation — Defense acquisition analysis
- IBM Institute for Business Value — AI in government procurement
- Deloitte Center for Government Insights — Public sector technology trends
