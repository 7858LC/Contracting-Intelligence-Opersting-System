# Competitive Analysis — Procurement Intelligence Landscape

## Competitive Positioning Map

The following analysis maps existing solutions against two axes:
- **Intelligence depth** (raw data → synthesized insight → recommended action)
- **Capture stage coverage** (market shaping → pursuit → proposal → debrief)

## Detailed Competitor Profiles

### Deltek GovWin IQ
**Category:** Market intelligence + CRM  
**Strengths:** Broadest opportunity database, established brand, strong agency relationships data  
**Weaknesses:** Data presentation without AI synthesis; no bid/no-bid decision support; no award simulation; outdated UI; expensive for what it delivers  
**Price point:** ~$15,000–$60,000/year depending on modules  
**Verdict:** The incumbent to displace. Strong data, weak intelligence layer.

### Bloomberg Government
**Category:** News + procurement data  
**Strengths:** Real-time news, strong on policy and legislative tracking, quality analysis content  
**Weaknesses:** Not a capture tool; no workflow integration; content-heavy not action-oriented  
**Price point:** ~$8,000–$25,000/year  
**Verdict:** Complementary data source, not a platform competitor.

### HigherGov
**Category:** Market intelligence (modern UI)  
**Strengths:** Clean interface, good NAICS/agency analytics, growing fast  
**Weaknesses:** Data visualization without AI synthesis; no decision support; no workflow  
**Price point:** ~$5,000–$15,000/year  
**Verdict:** Best-designed data tool in the space. Not a Procurement Intelligence platform.

### GovTribe
**Category:** Contract award analytics  
**Strengths:** Strong on award history, incumbent identification, pricing benchmarks  
**Weaknesses:** Narrow scope; no capture workflow; no AI synthesis  
**Price point:** ~$3,000–$10,000/year  
**Verdict:** Point solution for a specific intelligence need. Acquirable.

### Salesforce Government Cloud
**Category:** CRM  
**Strengths:** Enterprise-grade CRM, broad adoption, deep integration ecosystem  
**Weaknesses:** No procurement-specific intelligence; requires heavy customization; expensive to implement  
**Price point:** $150–$300/user/month  
**Verdict:** Pipeline management, not capture intelligence. Different category.
